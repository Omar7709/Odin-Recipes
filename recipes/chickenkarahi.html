Chicken Karahi

Description

Chicken Karahi, or kadai chicken, is a dish that originates from the northern and northwestern parts of South Asia. The dish is prepared in a karahi, a type of wok often used in South Asian cooking. It can take between 30 and 50 minutes to prepare and cook the dish. It is usually served with naan, roti or rice. This is another famous dish which is an asian staple. It is also liked to be made during festivals and special occasions

Ingredients

  • 1kg chicken
  • red chillies
  • green chillies
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 onions

Steps

  1. Fry the chicken in cooking oil until its reddish brown
  2. add the tomatoes and let them free until they have turned to pulp
  3. chop and add the onion and let it cook with the stew
  4. add all the chillies and the garnishes
  5. cook for 1o minutes or until the chicken is tender
  6. serve with traditional roti, naan bread or rice
