Chicken Biryani

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating in theIndian Subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken being a popular preference), and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes. Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in South Asia, as well as among the diaspora from the region. It is a dish of choice prepared prepared for special occasions including festivals and family reunions. Some claim it was a royal dish in the mughal era as essentially it is a mixture of heavy chicken stew and rice. Back in the days not all could afford a lavish food like this

Ingredients

  • 500g White Rice
  • 1kg Chicken (can be boned or boneless)
  • 2-3 Red chillies
  • 2-3 Green chillies
  • 3 Tablespoons yoghurt
  • 2 Tablespoon Salt
  • 2 Tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 pieces of cinnamon
  • A few cloves
  • A few cardamoms

Steps

  1. fry the chicken in some oil until its reddish brown
  2. add all the spices, the salt and the ginger-garlic paste
  3. add the yoghurt and mix well. Keep this mixture aside for the time being
  4. boil the white rice layer them over the mixture we just made
  5. let our rice and stew mixture cook for sometime then serve with mint sauce and salad
